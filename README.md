# 🐦 FLARPY BLORB

A cozy little Flappy Bird-style game featuring a chaotic round bird who refuses to fly in a straight line.

Tap **Space** to flap, dodge pipes, and survive as long as possible before Blorb meets an untimely end.

**Simple to learn. Weirdly hard to put down.**

## ✨ Features

- One-button flap controls (**Space bar**)
- Random pipe spawning
- Persistent high score saving
- Animated wing flaps
- Drifting cloud background
- Instant restarts

---

## 🕹️ How to Play

- Press **Space** to flap upward
- Avoid hitting the pipes
- Avoid hitting the ground
- Beat your high score

That’s it. That’s the whole game.
